Foodwaste.

Food waste still happens at the production, purchase, and storage levels. A lot of food is purchased by consumers on impulse or excess, only to rot away in refrigerators. Sometimes, retailers throw food away because items do not meet the cosmetic criteria or because of overstock. Further, some discard food after misinterpreting expiration date labels whereas such food is still edible. All of these things combined lead to a lot of food being wasted, putting environmental resources under pressure and heightening global hunger.
