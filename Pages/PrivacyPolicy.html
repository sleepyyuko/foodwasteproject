Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your website collects, uses, and discloses information when you visit and interact with our website.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

As a personal home-based website primarily focused on sharing informadtion about food waste reduction, we aim to collect as little personal data as possible. However, certain types of information may be collected automatically or if you choose to provide it:

1. Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

When you visit our website, certain non-personal information may be automatically collected by our web hosting provider or through standard web server logs. This information typically includes:

  • Browser Type: The type of web browser you are using (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
  • Operating System: The operating system of your device (e.g., Windows, macOS, Android, iOS).
  • IP Address: Your Internet Protocol address (which can indicate your general location).
  • Referring Pages: The web page you visited before coming to our site.
  • Pages Visited: The pages you view on our site.
  • Time and Date of Visit: The date and time you accessed our site.

This information is aggregated and used for analytical purposes to understand how visitors interact with our website, improve its performance, and detect potential security issues. It does not directly identify you.

2. Personal Information (If Provided Voluntarily)

Generally, we do not require you to provide any personal information to use our website. However, if we were to introduce features in the future, such as:

  • Contact Forms: If you use a contact form to send us a message, we would collect your name and email address, along with the content of your message, to respond to your inquiry.
  • Newsletter Sign-ups: If we offer a newsletter, we would collect your email address to send you updates.
  • Comments Section: If we implement a comments section, we might collect your name/username and email address along with your comment.

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website's content and functionality.
  • To understand visitor demographics and interests to tailor content (non-personal data).
  • To respond to your inquiries if you contact us (personal data, if provided).
